Who are we?

GaLife.org is a Georgia based community organization that helps spread awareness of the importance of life insurance. Life insurance is an essential building block in establishing financial security and protecting families. We feel its vitally important that everyone owns a life insurance policy and becomes educated on its benefits. Everyone's situation is different, and often times can be complex, so we strongly recommend getting in touch with a professional. Completing our quote form will put you in touch with a professional employed by an industry leading company that will be able to answer all of your questions, evaluate your situation, and recommend solutions. In the mean time or if you're not quite ready to speak with a professional, visit our Get informed page to view a variety of articles and other content that will help you become educated in the world of life insurance if you're not already

Protect the ones you love most

With death ends life, but financial responsibilities remain. Life insurance is a blessing that gives us the opportunity to provide for our loved ones long after we're gone. Whether its covering funeral expenses or making sure your spouse and children aren't subjected to financial hardship, the variety of life insurance products on the market offer solutions for everyone.
